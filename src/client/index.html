About The App

01

As a scuba diver, we all share two similar problems, how can I keep track of where I dove, and where are some new places that I can dive? Whether it is forgetting your logbook or researching on the internet new spots to dive, we have all asked ourselves these questions. This platform will help you answer these questions with the touch of a button.

02

The goal of the Diver Now app is to create a platform that will connect the entire scuba dive community and make advancements toward the future. This application will allow divers to: record their dives at the exact site, share their dive experiences with the Diver Now community, help people discover new dive locations, and make the overall experience of exploring the underwater world effortless and enjoyable.

03

By having a user friendly platform, the process of researching new dive resorts and charter boats has never been easier. The app will allow dive businesses to promote their services and show their potential guest what an experience at their facility would be like and what their potential dive sites would be.

04

Creating this application has been a goal of mine for several years and I would not be able to do it without Diver Now's amazing team of developers. As the founder of Diver Now, Peter Tsakonas, I would like to thank the developers Randy Paquette, Jack Hueber, and Charles Hetterich for the countless hours they have put into helping this dream come alive. I would also like to thank David Menachem for the logo designs used within the app. The team will be adding new features and updates as time goes on in order to make the experience on the Diver Now app even more enjoyable.

See The Underwater World
With Your Own Eyes

With an easy-to-use, intuitive user interface, Diver Now allows you to add your favorite photos to your dive site, help create a visual of the site for other divers to see and help put a visual back in your mind of your best diving memories.

Have you ever seen clear Florida spring water mix with brown river water? Ever wonder what lays beneath that bottomless blue water in Greece? What about diving into history with a World War 2 wreck dive? Check out others divers experiences at these different sites and create a plan for your next diving adventure.
